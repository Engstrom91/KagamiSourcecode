About Kagami

This is our mission:

Kagami conducts and promotes research and analysis of European policy affairs. Kagami strives to act as a link between the academic world and policy-makers at various levels. By publishing reports and arranging seminars and conferences, Kagami aims to further stimulate research on the future of Europe. Contact us for more info. It's very important for us that we keep working to:

  • Promote ideas that can lead to better environmental policies
  • Contributing to an open society
  • Bring research from theory to practice

My mission

I am a writer and analyst with my own company. Current assignments include as Senior Adviser at the Swedish Institute for European Policy Studies (SIEPS), for the European Council on Foreign Relations (ECFR), for the Global Challenges Foundation (GCF) and for Stockholm Environment Institute (SEI). I am also an IEEP Associate. My professional career includes positions in the Swedish government services, inter alia as Deputy State Secretary and Director of the International Departement at the Ministry for the Environment, and as Political Adviser to Foreign Minister Anna Lindh 1998-2001. I have also been Editorial Writer at the newspaper Aftonbladet for seven years, Editor-in-Chief of Ny Teknik, the leading technology magazine in Sweden, and Policy Director at the biggest Swedish trade union, Unionen.
